About the Event

We welcome you to join us for a community event that is set in nature and is all about compassionate connection, playfulness, empowerment, knowledge sharing, exploring new ways of community-building and finding like-minded people. We offer different workshops for all levels of dept and connection and there will be a fair where you can find arts and crafts by (local) queer artists/ crafters. There will be a chill out space where you can listen simultaneously to relaxing music, some active workshops and some panel conversations. We will also host a mutual aid kitchen for which the proceeds will go to a Palestinian cause. We hope to see you there!
